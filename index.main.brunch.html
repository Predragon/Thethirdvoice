Born from Resilience

The Third Voice was created in just 11 days by a solo founder with no programming experience, using only a cellphone from inside immigration detention.

When human connection felt most distant, the vision for deeper, more empathetic communication became crystal clear. This app represents the power of human resilience and the belief that technology can bring us closer together, not further apart.

Every line of code, every feature, every word was crafted with the understanding that communication is the bridge between hearts and minds.
